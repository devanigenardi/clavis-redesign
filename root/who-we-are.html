We are business builders first, investors second.

In 2013, Clavis set out to build a different type of investment group. One that believes that operators don’t just make better partners—they build better businesses.

Clavis Capital Partners is a private investment firm with deep experience in investing in, operating and growing US based industrial businesses. We provide flexible capital to make control investments in a broad range of unique industrial oriented companies that have built strong corporate cultures and are at an inflection point in their growth path.

We ask a lot out of the companies we invest in. We demand a lot out of ourselves as well. We operate Clavis in an open, collaborative and ethical manner and have a win-win oriented culture.
